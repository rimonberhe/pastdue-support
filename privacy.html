Privacy Policy

PastDue

Overview

PastDue is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Storage

All task data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to external servers.

Data Collection

PastDue does not collect any personal data, usage data, or analytics. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking.

Third-Party Services

PastDue does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect data.

Notifications

PastDue uses local notifications to remind you about upcoming and overdue tasks. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and no data is sent externally.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support.echo498@silomails.com.
